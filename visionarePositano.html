Work Surfaces that Rise to Your Expectations

The refractive unit’s table comfortable fits any model slit lamp. Reduce specialist and patient fatigue by raising and lowering the height of the table to meet their heights. The table is high-quality, which has excellent characteristics such as wearing-resistance and corrosion-resistance.

Table Rotation

The tabletop rotates 90˚ for better adjustment and to swiftly move the unit out of the way for use of other equipment. An internal locking mechanism prevents unwanted movement once the table is set at its desired location.

Elegant Overhead Light

Attached overhead lamp provides increased luminosity for external exam, near refraction and overall comfort. The lamp can be controlled through the unit control panel.



A moveable phoropter arm supports manual and digital models alike. A compensation and lock feature aids in moving and steadying the machine. Thanks to this feature we provide comfort to the specialist and avoid muscle fatigue.

A fixed socket (AUX 2) supplies power to the chart projector to use in testing visual acuity and refraction. For your convenience, power is controlled directly from the control panel.

Additionally, a third auxiliary arm houses a small tray to hold drops, instruments and other tools needed throughout the exam so you can stay where you need to be for the patient.

For maximum comfort, the arms of the chair move back and forth and the articulated head rest adjusts to several positions for an individualized patient experience.

Foot-switch

When both hands are needed for exam procedures, adjust heights and reclination of the chair by a fully functional foot pedal.

Two control Panels

Control the elevation of both the slit lamp table and the patient chair with conveniently placed push buttons. A separate control panel modifies the brightness of the attached overhead lamp and all of the directional positions of the patient exam chair including a Return button that automatically brings the chair back to the original settings.

Recharging Wells

Charge handheld ophthalmoscopes, retinoscopes and otoscopes with a conveniently placed three-welled dock located within the center of the base.
